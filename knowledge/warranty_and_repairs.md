# Oster Warranty and Repairs

## Limited Warranty
Most Oster products come with a **1-year limited warranty** covering:
- Manufacturing defects
- Mechanical failures under normal use

## What Is Not Covered
- Damage caused by misuse or accidents
- Normal wear and tear
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications

## Warranty Claims
To file a warranty claim, customers must provide:
- Proof of purchase
- Product model number
- Description of the issue

## Repairs
Some products are eligible for repair rather than replacement. Repair eligibility is determined after troubleshooting with customer support.
