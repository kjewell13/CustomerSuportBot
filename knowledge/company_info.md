# Oster – Company Information

## About Oster
Oster is a consumer appliance brand known for kitchen and home products such as blenders, coffee makers, toasters, and hair grooming tools. The company focuses on durable, affordable appliances designed for everyday household use.

## Parent Company
Oster is a brand owned by Newell Brands, a global consumer goods company.
