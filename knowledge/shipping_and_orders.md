# Shipping and Order Information

## Order Processing
Orders are typically processed within **1–2 business days** after purchase.

## Shipping Options
- Standard Shipping: 5–7 business days
- Expedited Shipping: 2–3 business days (additional cost)

## Tracking Orders
Once an order ships, customers receive a confirmation email with a tracking number.

## Order Issues
For issues such as:
- Missing items
- Incorrect products
- Delayed shipments

Customers should contact customer support with their **order ID** for faster assistance.
