# Oster Return Policy

## Standard Return Policy
Oster accepts returns for most products within **30 days of purchase**.

To be eligible for a return:
- The item must be unused or lightly used
- Original packaging is preferred
- Proof of purchase is required

## Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned:
- Personal grooming products that have been used
- Clearance or final sale items
- Gift cards

## Defective Products
Defective products may qualify for replacement instead of a refund. Customers should contact support before returning a defective item.
